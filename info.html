Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my cat to the gallery?

Simply log in to your account, click the "Add Cat" button, and fill in your cat's details including their name, tag, and a cute photo!

Is the Cats Gallery free to use?

Yes! Browsing and adding cats to our gallery is completely free. We believe everyone should have access to cat cuteness.

Can I edit my cat's profile?

Absolutely! You can update your cat's information at any time by clicking the "Edit" button on their card.
